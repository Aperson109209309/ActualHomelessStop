Arizona Gives Day: Double Your Impact to End Homelessness

Arizona Gives Day is just around the corner, and we're thrilled to announce a dollar-for-dollar match from an anonymous donor for all donations made on the Arizona Gives platform, now through April 1st, up to $10,000!

A report from the Maricopa Association of Governments revealed a heartbreaking statistic: in 2023, 645 people died from heat-related causes—a staggering 52% increase from 2022—with 45% of these victims being homeless. Many of these tragedies occurred near the Key Campus, where homelessness has risen by 46% since 2019.

With vulnerable individuals facing life-threatening conditions on the streets, now is the time to act. Please join us in supporting Keys to Change and help make a difference.
