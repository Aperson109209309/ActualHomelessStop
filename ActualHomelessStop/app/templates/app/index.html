Homeless Stop is co-hosting with Keys to Change on Thirst Aid, their summer-long heat relief campaign.

Together, we are collecting bottled water and other heat relief items for those who need them most.

Each summer, thousands of individuals experiencing homelessness rely on Key Campus, a collaborative space where many organizations (including us!) come together to provide life-saving services. During the hottest months, water is essential, but it is not always easy to access.

That’s where you come in..

Join us on September 6th at the Harrison Gate at Key Campus to donate. We’re collecting bottled water, new socks & underwear, hats, sunglasses, sunscreen, lip balm, travel-size toiletries, cooling towels, and lightweight summer clothing.

Every donation helps protect lives in the heat; it’s one of the easiest, tangible ways to help.
