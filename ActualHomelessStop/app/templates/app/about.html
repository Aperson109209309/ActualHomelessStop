We are a group of two high schoolers with a shared passion for helping the homeless.

In our efforts to help, we interviewed several local nonprofits. There, we learned that nonprofits were being restrained by a lack of funding and volunteers, limiting how much they could do.

That's why we created Homeless Stop, the one-stop shop where people like you can easily find, donate, volunteer, and contact nearby nonprofits.

We hope that our website will facilitate the process of helping nonprofits help the homeless. Together, let's stop homelessness, with Homeless Stop.
