I am Eric Wang, a high schooler with a passion for helping the homeless.

In my efforts to help, I interviewed several local nonprofits. There, I learned that nonprofits were being restrained by a lack of funding and volunteers, limiting how much they could do.

That's why I created Homeless Stop, the one-stop shop where people like you can easily find, donate, volunteer, and contact nearby nonprofits.

I hope that my website will facilitate the process of helping nonprofits help the homeless. Together, let's stop homelessness, with Homeless Stop.
