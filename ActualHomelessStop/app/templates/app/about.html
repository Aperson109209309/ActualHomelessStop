Homelessness is a pressing issue across the world, with an estimated 150 million homeless people, and that number is rising higher and higher every year.

There are countless nonprofit organizations working to combat homelessness, but they are limited by a lack of funding and volunteers.

Homeless Stop seeks to empower these nonprofits by streamlining the process for people to donate, volunteer, and learn about nonprofits that help the homeless.

Together, let's stop homelessness, with Homeless Stop.
